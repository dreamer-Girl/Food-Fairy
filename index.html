About Us



75% of wasted food is untouched and very much healthy for consumption




It's not the unwillingness of people to share that causes food to get wasted but it's the gap between the people who need food and the people who want to share it.

So here's where Food Fairy comes into the picture. We make use of the technology at our disposal, so that its no longer a hassle for people to do a good deed.

All you need to do search the nearest active organization around you and after giving us all the required information your package will get picked. Hassle Free Right!!! .
